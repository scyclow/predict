F.A.Q.s

Are any of the NUMBER_COINs financial securities?
No. The market values of $ONE, $TWO, $THREE, $FOUR, and $FIVE are driven by pure speculative energy, and no holder has a reasonable expectation of profit.
How do I win a prize?
In order to win a prize, you must predict the number that the NUMBER_PICKER will pick and buy the corresponding coin. Once a number has been picked, you can claim 0.0001 ETH per winning coin you own. For example, if you purchase 10,000 $THREE coins and 3 is picked, you can exchange your $THREE coins for 1 ETH. Alternatively, if 4 is picked instead, you will not win a prize.
Which number will the NUMBER_PICKER pick?
The NUMBER_PICKER will pick a number (or delegate number picking responsibilities) at their sole discretion. Once a number is picked it may not be changed, and all holders of the winning coin may claim their prize. The NUMBER_PICKER may arbitrarily change their mind about which number shall be picked up until the moment that the number picking transaction has cleared.
When will the NUMBER_PICKER pick a number?
The NUMBER_PICKER will pick a number on an undisclosed future date at their sole discretion.
Who is the NUMBER_PICKER?
You can view the current NUMBER_PICKER by viewing the $PREDICT contract and calling the NUMBER_PICKER() function. Initially, this is the deployer of the contract. However, the NUMBER_PICKER role may be transferred to another individual or smart contract at a later date.
Can the NUMBER_PICKER pick another number?
No. The NUMBER_PICKER can only pick 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5.
Will the NUMBER_PICKER ensure the number is picked fairly?
No. The NUMBER_PICKER can arbitrarily pick any number they want at their full discretion.

$ Be a Market Maker! $

  • Market Makers ensure that the markets for each of the five NUMBER_COINs are liquid and efficient. They do this by creating and redeeming NUMBER_COINs in exchange for the corresponding PRIZE_VALUE.
  • At any point before a number is picked, Market Makers can create a basket of NUMBER_COINs. This can be done by depositing ETH into the prize pool. NUMBER_COINs may be created in any quantity. Depositing 0.0001 ETH will return a basket containing one of each NUMBER_COIN ($ONE, $TWO, $THREE, $FOUR, and $FIVE).
  • Every time a Market Maker creates a new basket of NUMBER_COINs, they are also given a corresponding amount of $PREDICT (Predict Market Maker) coins.
  • Before a number is picked, Market Makers may redeem a basket of NUMBER_COINs and $PREDICT for the equivalent PRIZE_VALUE. For example, a Market Maker would receive 1 ETH by redeeming a basket containing 10,000 $ONE, 10,000 $TWO, 10,000 $THREE, 10,000 $FOUR, 10,000 $FIVE, and 10,000 $PREDICT. NUMBER_COINs and $PREDICT may be redeemed in any quantity.
  • ARBITRAGE OPPORTUNITY #1: If the summation of all five NUMBER_COIN market prices exceeds 0.0001 ETH Market Makers can arbitrage the price by creating coins at a lower price than what they can be sold for.
  • ARBITRAGE OPPORTUNITY #2: If the summation of all five NUMBER_COIN market prices is lower than 0.0001 ETH Market Makers can arbitrage the price by redeeming coins at a higher price than what they can be bought for.
